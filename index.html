What is Alien Ramen?

Part restaurant sim, part intergalactic adventure. Serve up bowls of cosmic ramen to hungry aliens from across the universe before they take matters into their own tentacles.

🌌

Cosmic Setting

Run your noodle shop aboard a space station at the crossroads of the galaxy. Every customer brings a story from a distant star system.

🍥

Ramen Crafting

Experiment with alien ingredients and interdimensional broths to discover hundreds of unique ramen recipes that satisfy the most exotic palates.

👽

Quirky Characters

Meet a cast of eccentric alien regulars, each with their own cravings, backstories, and universe-sized personalities.
